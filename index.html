The International Programs & Collaborations Office at OCAD University presents this year's International Lecture Series, Beyond Bias: Building inclusive global creative collaborations.

This international lecture series brings together academics and researchers from around the world to share their experiences, methodologies, and scholarly approaches to fostering inclusive collaborations across borders. Focusing on the intersection of global education and creative research, the series aims to provide valuable insights on how to collaborate effectively with both students and professors from diverse cultural backgrounds.
